Finances


Origin of financial resources


1. PROAP

The main financial source of the Graduate Program in Language Studies of the UFF comes from the Post-Graduate Program-Proap-CAPES. In the year 2013, this amount is R $ 59,200.00 distributed in 12 quotas.            With this source, the Post-Graduate Program in Language Studies supports:
a) The participation of members from outside the state of Rio de Janeiro in the thesis defense thesis;
b) The participation of occasional collaborators, visiting professors from Brazil and abroad, with a view to the realization of mini-courses, conferences and other academic events (each line of research evemtps and a mini-course with this support);
c) The participation of teachers of the Program in a national event of the area, with passages or daily (the Program supports, preferably with airfare, all teachers, except those who have a grant from CNPq, in a national event per year);
d) The participation of students of the Program in a national event of the area, with road and lodging (each advisor indicates one student per year, master's or doctorate);
e) The publication of works contemplated in the Edict of the Ensaios Collection, destined to the dissemination of results of research of teachers and students of the Program;
f) The payment of service providers, with a view to maintaining the program page and other technical works, such as editorial review, typing, among others;
g) The purchase of equipment, permanent and consumer material, in the viability of the continuous bureaucratic activities necessary for the proper functioning of the Program.

2. SOURCE 250

This budget item refers to the amount obtained through the payment of enrollment of the selection exam to the masters and doctorate courses of the Program. Each year, according to the terms of the respective notice for the said selection, the candidates pay, through a deposit in the account of the UFF, a registration fee in the amount of R $ 100.00. Unlike Proap / Capes, whose 12 quotas must be spent by the Program on schedule, the resources of Source 250 can be accumulated and used later, even from year to year.            For 2013, the balance of Source 250, excluding university percentages, is R $ 33,000.00 This amount is used for the same types of expenditure as Proap / Capes.

3. Research scholarship

In 2014, in the fourth year of its operation, the Program registers 79 research fellowship quotas for its student body, as follows:
a) CAPES: 24 Masters degrees and 21 Doctorate degrees
b) CNPq: 8 Masters degrees and 6 Doctorate degrees
c) REUNI: 7 Masters degrees e 8 Doctorate degrees
d) FAPERJ: 1 Masters degrees e 2 de Doutorado
e) PAEC/OEA - 1 Master degrees

4. PROPPI/EDUFF

The Program has obtained financial resources for the publication, in printed version, of 500 copies of each of the two annual issues of the Revista Gragoatá, entitled Qualis, published by the UFF and UFF's Editors and Research Pro-Rectory. The National. This is an important grant, which aims to maintain the standards of excellence and guarantee periodicity of this publication, which is a reference in the area of Letters and Linguistics throughout the national territory.            Through the EdUFF Library Edict, the program's faculty members have published their research results.

5. EDITAIS PROPPI/UFF

In the scope of the UFF's Pro-Rector's Office for Research, Graduate and Innovation, there are four annual edicts in which teachers and students have obtained funds for funding, dissemination of their research in national and international events in the area and publication of the results of these researches. The mentioned edicts are the following:

a. EDITAL FLUXO CONTÍNUO: intended to support, with up to R $ 2,000.00 per year, the individual research of teachers or groups of the Program that do not have a grant from CNPq; Such support can be through tickets and daily for events, purchase of permanent equipment, consumer equipment, among other items. The UFF research group leaders registered at CNPq can also request support of up to R $ 5,000 for event organization and others.

b. EDITAL INFRA-PG: This new support of the UFF is exclusively destined to the "stricto sensu" graduate programs and is directed to the improvement of its conditions of physical and academic infrastructure; Upon submission of a single annual project, the coordination may request up to R $ 5.00 per year.

c. EDITAL PRÓ-ALUNO: Aimed at the student body, this support enables the participation of masters and doctoral students in national events in the area, with presentation of research work; As a result of a successful partnership between the Pro-Rector's Office for Graduate Studies, the Academic Pro-Rector's Office and the Pro-Rector's Office of Planning, this notice allows, through the subsidy of road tickets and accommodation, Of the research developed at UFF both in undergraduate and postgraduate studies.

d. EDITAL AUXÍLIO-PUBLICAÇÃO: The purpose of this proposal is to partially replace the costs of publishing articles, chapters and books of the researcher, as well as to signal the recognition of the UFF in relation to the work of its scientists, at the same time, serving as a stimulus to the UFF.

6. OTHER SOURCES

a) PROINFRA/FINEP

The "Books and Readings in the XXI Century: Multidisciplinary Research Network of the Human Sciences Area" subproject, selected and forwarded by PROPP, as an integral part of the UFF's institutional project in PROINFRA / FINEP Notice, was granted substantial funding for the purchase Of books and the improvement of the entire infrastructure of the Central Library of Gragoatá (BCG). In this subproject, the Program associates itself with the other graduate programs stricto sensu of the Human Sciences area of the UFF, aiming at the expansion and maintenance of the bibliographic collection of each of these programs, as well as to the increase of BCG conditions, in Physical and computer terms.

b) Editais FAPERJ

The Foundation for Research Support of the State of Rio de Janeiro launches several announcements of interest for the Program, in which the faculty has been contemplated. Among the calls for papers, APQ1 is dedicated to the granting of groups and research projects; The CI, aimed at granting scholarships to undergraduate students; (APQ3), for the publication of works of renowned academic merit of state researchers, the Scientists of Our State, which recently included two teachers of the Program, Lucia Teixeira and Bethânia Mariani, among other initiatives.
