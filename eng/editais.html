CAPES PDSE 2017

In compliance with what was decided at today's collegiate meeting, we forwarded the instructions for the applications for the scholarship program of the Sandwich Overseas Doctoral Program (PDSE) in accordance with the CAPES Call Notice 19/2016, edict already forwarded by this coordination in July, With the changes in items 4.1.1; 5.5; 5.5.3, 5.6; 5.6.1; 5.6.5, also forwarded in August.

In line with what was decided by PosLing's Board of Directors, we request that they be delivered by september 26 , Next Monday, in the secretariat of the post-graduation, room 518 of Block C, until 17hrs the following documents:

  1. Candidate's updated Lattes curriculum LATTES;
  2. Letter from the Brazilian advisor, duly signed and on letterhead of the UFF, justifying the need for the internship and demonstrating technical-scientific interaction with the overseas co-orientator for the development of the proposed activities;
  3. Summarized curriculum of the coorientador abroad, which must have compatible scientific and / or technological production and the minimum degree of doctorate;
  4. Term of selection of candidacy for the PDSE, according to the model available in the CAPES Call for Proposals, on the Agency's page;
  5. Plan of studies in Portuguese, with a maximum of 15 pages, with a schedule of the activities plan, contemplating the provisions in item 5.4.3, paragraph IX of the aforementioned Notice.
  6. Approval in qualification examination;
  7. Deadline for completion of doctoral degree;
  8. Candidates's curriculum.

We clarify that the collegiate decided to abide by the criteria established by the scholarship committee of the program, which will guide the work of the commission that will select the candidates. Are they:

I - in relation to the candidate

  1. Approval in qualification examination;
  2. Deadline for completion of doctoral degree;
  3. Candidates's curriculum.

II - in relation to the institution of destination:

  1. Academic cooperation with the PPG in Language Studies;
  2. Foreign supervisor's curriculum.
