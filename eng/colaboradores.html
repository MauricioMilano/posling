The Interinstitutional Doctorate (DINTER) offered by the Graduate Program in Language Studies of UFF to the Federal Institute of Education, Science and Technology of the Southeast of Minas Gerais (IF Sudeste MG), implemented according to a project approved by CAPES in 2014, Began in August 2015, with the enrollment of 16 PhD students, all of whom were teachers or technicians from the Southeast MG.


Created in 2008, the IF Sudeste MG has eight campuses - Barbacena, Bom Sucesso, Juiz de Fora, Manhuaçu, Muriaé, Pomba River, Santos Dumont and São João Del-Rei - and offers technical and higher education, postgraduate, research And extension, in addition to short-term and distance learning courses. The Doctorate in Language Studies course, which we offer to the professionals of this Institute through DINTER, has among its main objectives to train qualified personnel of a high professional level for the exercise of research activities and / or higher education, as well as contribute For the advancement of knowledge, in the context of language studies.
